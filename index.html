Advanced Manufacturing Solutions

01
VMC MACHINING

State-of-the-art Vertical Machining Centers for complex 3D geometries, multi-feature parts, and high-precision surface finishes on any axis.

  • Travel: X-1100 × Y-600 × Z-600mm
  • Spindle: up to 15,000 RPM
  • ATC: 30+24 tool magazine
  • Repeatability: ±0.002mm
02
CNC TURNING

High-speed CNC lathes delivering precise cylindrical components, shafts, flanges, and complex turned parts with live tooling and Y-axis capability.

  • Max Diameter: Ø500mm
  • Max Length: 2000mm
  • Live tooling Y-axis milling
  • Surface Finish: Ra 0.4μm
03
5-AXIS MACHINING

Simultaneous 5-axis for aerospace impellers, turbine blades, and intricate sculptured geometries — impossible to achieve with 3-axis, done in one setup.

  • Rotary: ±120° A, 360° C-axis
  • Positional Accuracy: 0.001°
  • Complex contouring
  • Single setup, full part
04
WIRE EDM

Electrical discharge machining for ultra-precise profiles, complex cutouts and keyways through any electrically conductive material regardless of hardness.

  • Wire Diameter: 0.1–0.3mm
  • Accuracy: ±0.003mm
  • Max cut height: 300mm
  • 4-axis taper cutting
05
CMM INSPECTION

Zeiss CMM coordinate measuring machines ensuring 100% dimensional compliance with full GD&T reporting, First Article Inspection, and PPAP documentation.

  • Range: 700×1000×600mm
  • Accuracy: 1.2μm
  • Full GD&T reporting
  • FAIR / PPAP / FAI docs
06
SURFACE GRINDING

Precision surface and cylindrical grinding for mirror-finish surfaces and ultra-tight flatness tolerances on critical mating and sealing components.

  • Table: 600×300mm
  • Flatness: 0.001mm/300mm
  • Surface finish: Ra 0.2μm
  • CNC-controlled dressing

Our Manufacturing Process

01 // DFM REVIEW
DESIGN FOR MANUFACTURABILITY

Engineering team reviews CAD/CAM files, identifies optimization, confirms feasibility and provides DFM feedback before quoting.

02 // CAM
TOOLPATH GENERATION

Expert programmers create optimized G-code using Mastercam for maximum efficiency, surface quality, and tool life.

03 // SETUP
FIXTURING & WORKHOLDING

Custom fixtures and zero-point clamping systems ensure repeatable sub-micron accuracy across full production runs.

04 // MACHINE
PRECISION MANUFACTURING

Automated CNC/VMC machining with in-process gauging and statistical process control monitoring every cycle.

05 // QC
DIMENSIONAL VERIFICATION

CMM inspection, surface roughness measurement, and full material traceability documentation for every batch shipped.
